New Operators Announced

Lion and Finka, both attacking operators, have been revealed as part of Operation Chimera. Lion, a GIGN operator, boasts a meta-changing ability: the E.E.D. drone. This gadget, following a timer that shows on defender's screens, will produce a full-body red outline of any defending operator moving while the gadget is active. However, any defender in the radius of a mute jammer will not be detected. Finka, a Spetsnaz op, has the nanobot shot or adrenal surge, which gives all teammates a 20 hp boost during its duration as well as reducing recoil. If a teammate is in the downed but not out state, they will be revived with 25 health. Counters to her gadget include Pulse, who will be able to see heartbeats from a further distance; Smoke, whose gas canisters do significantly higher damage while the adrenaline rush is active; Lesion, whose G.U. mines will negate the buff when his gadget is triggered; and Echo, whose Yokai drone will also remove the buff when stunning enemies.
